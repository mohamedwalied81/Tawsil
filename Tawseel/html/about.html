Our Story

How Tawseel Began

Founded in 2020, Tawseel was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize transportation by making it more accessible, reliable, and comfortable for everyone.

Our founders noticed a significant gap in the transportation market â€“ the lack of a service that combined convenience, affordability, and quality. They embarked on a mission to create a solution that would address these challenges and transform the way people travel.

Starting with just two routes and a handful of buses, Tawseel has grown exponentially over the years. Today, we are proud to serve thousands of passengers daily, connecting major cities with our modern fleet of over 200 buses.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and comfortable transportation services that exceed customer expectations while contributing positively to the communities we serve.

Our Vision

To be the leading transportation provider in the region, known for excellence in service, innovation in solutions, and commitment to sustainability.
